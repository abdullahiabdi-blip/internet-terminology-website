🏢 Intranet

An intranet is a private computer network that uses internet technologies but is accessible only within an organization.

Key features:

  • Private and secure - only employees can access
  • Used for internal communication and collaboration
  • Hosts company documents, HR portals, and internal tools
  • Not accessible from the public internet

Examples of intranet use: Employee directories, company news, internal forms, training materials.

Intranets improve productivity by centralizing company information.
