🧠 Web 4.0 (The Intelligent Web)

Web 4.0 represents the future of the internet where AI, IoT, and ubiquitous computing create a seamless digital-physical world.

Characteristics of Web 4.0:

  • AI-Powered: Artificial intelligence drives personalization and automation
  • Symbiotic Web: Humans and machines work together intelligently
  • Ubiquitous Computing: Internet is everywhere - wearables, smart homes, cars
  • Voice & Gesture Control: Beyond typing and clicking
  • Predictive Intelligence: Systems anticipate your needs before you ask
  • Seamless Integration: Digital and physical worlds merge

Examples of Web 4.0: AI assistants (like Copilot), smart cities, autonomous vehicles, brain-computer interfaces, Web of Things.

Web 4.0 aims to create an intelligent, responsive, and connected ecosystem that feels like an extension of human thought.
